How Does A Gaming Payment Gateway Work?

A gaming payment gateway is a crucial component in the digital gaming ecosystem, facilitating transactions between players and game providers.

1
A client adds games to the cart or just selects an item to purchase (depending on the gaming platform interface structure).

3
A payment page with the purchase details opens. It includes a merchant's wallet address for transfer and the purchase amount in the selected currency.
4
The client sends the payment.

The funds are transferred to the merchant's account after the transaction is verified in the blockchain.
