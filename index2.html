 Pros and cons of payment gateways

While selecting a cryptocurrency processing service for business, it’s vital to take into account several essential aspects.

Advantages of cryptocurrency payment gateways

  • 1.
    Global reach
    Expand your customer base by reaching wider geos with cryptocurrencies that are not tied to any specific country or currency.
  • 2.
    Lower Transaction Fees
    Unlike traditional payment methods, cryptocurrency payment gateways do not charge additional or hidden fees for processing transactions. You only pay a network fee which is several times lower than in fiat payment systems. It’s especially beneficial for cross-border transactions and international cooperation.
  • 3.
    High Transaction Speed
    It takes several seconds to process almost any transaction via a cryptocurrency payment gateway. Banks take up to several days to process transfers and even can freeze a transaction charging you additional fees for returns if one is canceled.
  • 4.
    Security
    Blockchain is decentralized which means no third parties taking part in processing payments. Due to encryption, the cashflows are more secure from fraudsters.

Disadvantages of cryptocurrency payment gateways

  • 1.
     Volatility
    There are some cryptocurrencies (e.g. Bitcoin, Ethereum) that are extremely volatile, so you should choose a stable option and reliable option, for example, USDT which is bent to the real Dollar rate.
  • 2.
    Security Risks
    Despite that the crypto transactions are encrypted and secure, your fund storage is still vulnerable to fraudsters and data leakage. To ensure the security of your funds, you should carefully select a payment gateway provider that utilizes all the necessary protection measures.

PassimPay pays special attention to funds and data security. We use DDoS protection, Two-factor authentication, IP tracking, login control, etc. With the PassimPay cryptocurrency gateway, your coins and user info are safe.
